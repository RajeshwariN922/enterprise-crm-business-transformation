# enterprise-crm-business-transformation

## Business Context
A mid-size enterprise is experiencing fragmented customer data,
manual workflows, and limited operational visibility across teams.

## Problem Statement
Business users struggle with inconsistent data, delayed case resolution,
and lack of reliable reporting, impacting operational efficiency
and stakeholder decision-making.

## Stakeholders
- Operations
- IT
- Customer Support
- Compliance
- Leadership

## Solution Overview
A Salesforce-based CRM solution was designed to standardize processes,
improve data quality, and enable operational reporting.
The solution focuses on scalable workflows, clear ownership,
and governance-friendly design.

## Key Artifacts in This Project
- Business requirements and user stories
- As-Is and To-Be process flows
- Salesforce solution design
- UAT approach and test scenarios
- Reporting and dashboard concepts

## Business Impact (Expected)
- Improved data consistency
- Reduced manual effort
- Faster issue resolution
- Better leadership visibility

## Assumptions & Constraints
- Existing legacy systems remain in place
- Data quality issues require phased cleanup
- Change management is critical for adoption
